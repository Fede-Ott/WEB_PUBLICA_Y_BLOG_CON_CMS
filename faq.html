FAQ of CalzeMania

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our collection, select your favorite socks, choose the right size, and add them to your cart. When you're ready, proceed to checkout and follow the steps to complete your purchase.

Our socks are crafted from premium materials to ensure comfort and durability:

  • Cotton-blend socks: 80% cotton, 15% polyester, 5% elastane
  • Merino wool socks: 70% merino wool, 25% nylon, 5% elastane
  • Athletic performance socks: 60% polyester, 35% cotton, 5% elastane

All materials are carefully selected for breathability, moisture management, and long-lasting wear.

To maintain the quality and longevity of your CalzeMania socks:

  • Machine wash cold with similar colors
  • Use mild detergent
  • Avoid bleach and fabric softeners
  • Tumble dry on low heat or air dry
  • Do not iron

For wool socks, we recommend washing by hand or using the wool/delicate cycle on your washing machine.

We offer a 90-day hassle-free return policy for all our products:

  • Free returns for orders over €30
  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive your return

To initiate a return, please visit your account page or contact our customer service team.
